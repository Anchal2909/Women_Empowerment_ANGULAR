Training Details

Agriculture

   

Course Details

About the course: Kisan Mitras would be trained resource persons who work with farmers to help them shift towards agro-ecological approaches to sustain their farming, organise themselves into producer cooperatives, understand the issues in public policy and improve the support systems. They will undergo a foundation course and can specialise in any of the areas of ecological farming, farmers’ institutions and marketing or public policy support.
Objective: Minimal literacy to read and write and communicate in Hindi/English and experience in working farmers is essential.
Fee: 800 /-
Duration: 50 days

Entrepreneurial Initiatives

   

Course Details

About the course: MSDE is committed to facilitate growth of women entrepreneurs in the country. NIESBUD under the MSDE has designed Entrepreneurship Development Programs for the rural women with the objective to inculcate entrepreneurial values, attitude and motivation among the Rural women to take up challenges to set up an enterprise/Group Enterprises. The Livelihood Business Incubation (LB I) approach is also used to promote woman entrepreneurs by the Institute.
Objective: The main object of this program is to make women independent and confident both economically and socially through the training.
Fee: 1200 /-
Duration: 60 days

Special Women-Centric Projects

   

Course Details

About the course: NSDC, through its training partners such as Mann Deshi Foundation, Shri Mahila Sewa Sahkari Bank Limited and Sri Sarada Math RasikBhita are working exclusively on skill development of women, especially in rural areas. The training constitutes imparting digital, accounting and entrepreneurial skills so as to facilitate the possibility of setting up their own business.
Objective: Encouraging young women to take informed career decisions. Initiate mobile training centers and digital learning centers for access to quality training leading to skill development.
Fee: 1200 /-
Duration: 60 days

Fashion Designing

   

Course Details

About the course: NSDC, through this initiative is working exclusively on skill development of women who want to become a successful Professional Fashion Designer. The training constitutes understanding fashion cycles, ,equipments,materials,design work and fashion drawing to facilitate the possibility of setting up their own business.
Objective: Providing an oppurtunity to aspiring women and young girls who are interested in fasion , an induction into exciting world of fashion .to take career in decisions.
Fee: 1700 /-
Duration: 90 days

Food Processing

   

Course Details

About the course: NSDC, through this training is offering viable options in food processing sector for women entrepreneurs, which could also be tapped by rural women. Participants get acquainted with a wide range of food processing methods for fruit beverages, jam, jelly, fancy cakes and cookies, value addition of traditional millets and rice and techniques for fermented food products.
Objective: Encouraging women to take informed career decisions. Providing them oppurunity to generate employment through small scale food processing hub , create self help groups and sustain their life.
Fee: 1000 /-
Duration: 45 days

Tailoring

   

Course Details

About the course: The trainees will be selected from the poor and destitute women of the society belonging low income group preferably below 40 years of age having primary knowledge of reading and writing. The trainees will get the stipend of Rs. 200/- per month. The duration of training is for one year. maximum number of trainees in each center is 50.
Objective: The main object of the scheme is to rehabilitate the poor and destitute girls and women of the society both economically and socially through the training in cutting and tailoring and knitting.
Fee: 1700 /-
Duration: 90 days

Stitching

   

Course Details

About the course: The beauty and expertise that Indians show in stitching and tailoring is admired by individuals across the globe. Specifically in India, there is a great diversity in the kinds of clothes people wear- each style is unique in its own way. Comprehending all these various styles is a challenge; with the challenge comes a great opportunity.
Objective: To teach the trainees on how to perform stitiching from basic and with time how can it be improved for the betterment and develop as a skill for earning.
Fee: 900 /-
Duration: 60 days
