MISSION AND VISION

Our Mission is to empower women with tools to work their way out of poverty, care for their families, and strengthen their communities. A world where every woman is able to use her voice, make her own decisions, and engage in economic opportunities.
We envision a world without poverty in which women are empowered, uplifted and equal partners in society.they possess the tools to provide a better life for themselves and their families and they have the hope, opportunity and ability to control their lives and futures. We believe all women deserve the opportunity to live free from poverty and being self-independent. We partner with high-impact grassroots ngos and organizations. together, we provide different trainings and courses that enable hard-working women to create sustainable livelihoods, start their own small scale businesses, support their families and strengthen their communities, improve their lives and change our world.
We’re funded 100% by your charitable donations.
